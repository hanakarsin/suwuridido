Obasanjo Reveals Presidential Aspirants Who Can Fix Nigeria

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Former Nigerian President Olusegun Obasanjo has opined that Nigeria needs men and women of integrity to improve the country’s economy.

Obasanjo stated this on Tuesday at the global crusade of the Deeper Life Bible Church in Abeokuta, the Ogun State capital.

According to Obasanjo Nigeria and the entire world needs God’s help and urged everyone to seek divine intervention.

The former Head of State said Noah stood out, as well as Job, whom God described as a man of great integrity.

“Nigeria needs such men of integrity at this present moment”.

Olusegun Obasanjo added that despite the current situation in Nigeria, everything will be well.

He said: “If you consider what is going on in our States, our country, West Africa, our continent and indeed our globe, our world requires a crusade of this nature”.

“I believe that because of men and women of such virtue in Nigeria, God will open his eyes and have mercy on this country”.

Meanwhile ahead of the 2023 election notable chieftains of the All Progressive Congress Party including the vice president, Yemi Osinbajo and Asiwaju Bola Tinubu have declared their intentions.
