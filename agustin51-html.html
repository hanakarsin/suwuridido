Agustin51 - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Spanish gamer and Fortnite expert who is famous for his Agustin51 channel's content. He has earned popularity there for his gaming montages, playthroughs, and commentary for a litany of titles, including Call of Duty: Black Ops and Clash Royale. In 2019, he joined the web group Escuadrón Salchichón.

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in June 2015. 

Trivia

He has amassed more than 5 million subscribers to his YouTube channel. In July 2019, he played for a YouTuber squad led by TheGrefg in a charity soccer match organized by Adidas, Real Madrid and YouTube Gaming. 

Family Life

His real name is Antonio. His younger brother JuliONline is also a gamer. 

Associated With

He's part of the team of influencers Escuadrón Salchichón alongside TheGrefg, Vicens and Ampeterby7, and they all live together in a house in Andorra.
