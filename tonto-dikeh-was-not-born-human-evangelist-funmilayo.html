Tonto Dikeh Was Not Born Human

Publish date: 2024-04-04

A clergy woman has revealed that popular Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh is not human by birth .

In a video which has gone viral on social media, the woman identified as Evangelist Funmilayo Adebayo informed her congregants at the Mountain of Solution Church in Ogun state that Tonto had gone far in her endeavours while also stating that the actress was not human by birth.

She also expressed sympathy for men who were getting involved with the actress in the name of what she called “jenjebe” which is translated in English as “Let me also have a taste of something.”

Naija News recalls that Tonto was recently involved in a scandal about her last relationship with Delta state politician, Prince Kpokpogri.

The relationship which lasted for a duration of three months had led to Prince praising Tonto’s ex-husband for putting up with her.

The politician also claimed that Tonto cheated on him from the  beginning of their relationship.

The actress later sued Kpokpogri for his allegations while stating that the he was in possession of various sex tapes of different women.

Naija News had earlier reported that Oromoni was allegedly beaten to a pulp by his fellow students in the school for refusing to join a cult.
