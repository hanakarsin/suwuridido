Who Will Vote When We Are All Killed?

Publish date: 2024-02-09

Popular Kannywood actress, Rahama Sadau has taken to social media to lament the spate of insecurity in Nigeria, most especially in the Northern region.

Naija News reports that Sadau took to her verified Instagram page on Saturday (today) to challenge all concerned Nigerians to take action in curbing the nation’s security challenges.

The actress said recent viral footage on social media shows the level of inhuman some people are in the country.

According to Sadau, losing Arewa (Northern region) is like losing the whole country.

“Idan kowa ya mutu Sai muga wanda zai zabe su,” (Lets see who will vote for them when everybody is dead”, Rahama Sadau asked in the Hausa language.

Her post reads: “The disturbing recent footage on social media is disheartening & inhumane. What did we really do as citizens of the country (Arewa most especially) to deserve such cruelty and disregard from our leaders?

“All we did was VOTED!! We are not calling out anybody but EVERYBODY at the top level. When will there be action on insecurity? When will our voices be heard? Our attention only got to be noticed after every 4 years. ENOUGH OF THAT!!

“This insecurity is fucking affecting EVERY home in Arewa. Hmm!! Never Wake a Sleeping Dog. Talaka a yau, zama a cikin gidansa ma tsoro yake bashi. I am speaking for myself and every person that feel affected!!

“I am calling out EVERYBODY with a voice, power & influence to contribute in tackling insecurity in Nigeria. Losing Arewa to banditry is like losing the whole nation. Idan kowa ya mutu Sai muga wanda zai zabe su. NO ONE IS SAFE UNTIL WE ARE SAFE …. ENOUGH OF THE BLOODSHED!!
