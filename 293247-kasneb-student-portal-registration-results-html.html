KASNEB student portal: registration, results, timetables, fees

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Certified Public Accountant (CPA) programs are some of the most sought-after courses in Kenya. Every business owner needs an accountant to help balance the books. For many years, KASNEB has been known and respected as the country's provider of superior-quality accounting courses. Unfortunately, a huge number of students still don't benefit from the existence of the KASNEB student portal. Read below about what the student portal contains and how it simplifies most activities.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

KASNEB is responsible for certifying candidates in accounts, finance, credit, governance, management, information technology, and many other disciplines. Most institutions of learning have digitalized their operations, and KASNEB has also embraced this trend, offering a one-stop shop for students to get essential services with ease.

KASNEB student portal

The KASNEB student portal is an online platform for KASNEB students to manage their enrolment and access resources. The portal provides various services and resources to aid students throughout their academic journey with KASNEB.

  • Through the portal, students can register for KASNEB examinations, update their personal information, and enrol for specific courses or exams.
  • Access to examination timetables and plan their study schedules.
  • Provide access to study materials such as syllabi and recommended textbooks.
  • Students can view their results through the portal.
  • Students can pay registration, exam fees, and other related charges.
  • Access any current KASNEB news and updates.

KASNEB student portal registration

Previously, KASNEB registration was done manually. There were KASNEB registration forms provided in all the centers which offered their courses, the national library centers and downloadable on their website.

Students wishing to register would pick these forms, fill them out manually, and send them to the headquarters. While this method of registration is still available, the new and simpler way to register is through their website portal. Here is how to register for the KASNEB student portal.

How to get KPSEA results online for Grade 6 students in Kenya

  • Visit the official KASNEB website.
  • At the top of the home page, click on the link "Student Portal".
  • This will open the portal page. Click on "Don't have an account? Sign up Now."
  • A registration form will pop up. Fill in all form fields to go to the next step.
  • Once you have done this, a verification link will be sent to your email, and KASNEB will notify you about the registration via phone.
  • Follow the link in the email to confirm your account. The phone message will have a wallet pin, which you should not share with anyone.
  • Once you have verified, repeat steps 1 and 2 and then log in to your account using your email and password.

    How do you get your KASNEB timetable?

    You can access the KASNEB timetable using the SMS option or the KASNEB student portal. Alternatively, you can download the KASNEB app on the Google Play Store. Follow the steps below to download your timetable.

  • Download the e-kasneb app from the Google Play Store.
  • Sign up as a student with a registration number and fill in your details as directed.
  • An activation link will be sent to your email.
  • Once you have activated your account, log in using your email and password.
  • Update your profile with the required details.
  • Attach a picture of your ID and a passport photo.
  • Once done, click on "Resources", then "Timetable", and then "Download Timetable".

    What are the fees charged by KASNEB?

    The KASNEB registration fee and associated costs are listed on the website's examination services section. They are also listed on the back of the registration form, examination entry/annual registration renewal form, and exemption application form.

    Where is KASNEB located, and does it have branches?

    KASNEB is located at KASNEB Towers, Hospital Road, Upper Hill, Nairobi. Its services are now available at the following Huduma Centres: Nairobi GPO, Nairobi Kibera, Nairobi Makadara, Kisumu, Kisii, Eldoret, Meru, Nyeri, and Mombasa.

    How to get KASNEB registration number?

    After successfully registering and paying the fees, you will receive your KASNEB registration number. The process involves creating an account on the KASNEB student portal and registering for your chosen examinations.

    How long does KASNEB take to verify the registration details after course registration?

    It takes a maximum of 24 hours to have your details verified. You will receive an SMS notification with the verification details.
