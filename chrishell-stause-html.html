Chrishell Stause - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-21

Actress best known for playing Amanda Dillon on All My Children, and later Jordan Ridgeway in Days of Our Lives. In 2019, she began starring on Netflix's Selling Sunset as the new agent in town.

Before Fame

In 2003, she graduated with a theatre degree from Murray State University.

Trivia

She is of Japanese and Spanish descent.

Family Life

She was engaged to Matthew Morrison until 2007. In 2017, she married actor Justin Hartley, but the couple divorced in 2019. She later dated dancer Keo Motsepe. She also dated Jason Oppenheim. In 2022, she began dating singer G Flip. 

Associated With

She co-starred with Thorsten Kaye on All My Children.
