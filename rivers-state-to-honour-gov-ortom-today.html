Rivers State To Honour Gov Ortom Today

Publish date: 2024-03-18

Rivers State Government is set to honour the Benue State Governor, Samuel Ortom with one of the highest honours in the state today.

The state Governor, Nyesom Wike will present the award which is called ‘Grand Service Star of Rivers State (GSSRS)’ to Governor Ortom.

Governor Wike on Friday described the Benue State Governor as a true friend of Rivers people who is trustworthy and dependable.

Naija News learnt that the award ceremony is scheduled to take place at Government House, Port Harcourt.

‘Ortom An Errand Boy To Gov Wike’ – APC

The Governor of Benue State, Samuel Ortom has been accused of abandoning his constitutional responsibilities of providing governance to support the political activities of the Rivers State Governor, Nyesom Wike.

The Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC) in Benue State, Austin Agada in a chat with Punch said Ortom had reduced himself to an errand boy to Governor Wike.

Agada noted that there had been setbacks in the state because Ortom abandoned the state to pursue his interest.

He said, “He (Ortom) had long ago attached himself to Wike; this is responsible for his abysmal performance in the past seven years. He has abandoned governance which is the reason everything is in total decay. He is a disaster to Benue State, he has become an errand boy or may I call him deputy to Wike or an aide to Wike; that is the reason you always see him going around with Wike.”
