VicBlends - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Barber who has gained fame for his VicBlends TikTok account. His short clips, POVs, and Q&As about growing his hair cutting business have helped him amass more than 12 million followers. 

Before Fame

He began cutting hair in high school as a side job. 

Trivia

He has shared his luxury cuts and hair styling to his vicblends Instagram account, where he has accumulated more than 1.6 million followers. He also posts vlogs to his VicBlends YouTube channel. 

Family Life

He has three siblings. His dad served in the military.

Associated With

He has styled Lil Baby, NLE CHOPPA, Polo G, Mozzy, 2kBaby, Nelly, D'Angelo Russell and Cam Reddish's hair, and featured the works on Instagram.
