Ryth - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-01

YouTube content creator and personality who rose to fame through his self-titled channel. He publishes pop and influencer culture commentary videos. He uses an animated purple cat as his avatar. He has more than 3.5 million subscribers on the platform.

Before Fame

He made his YouTube debut in 2021 with the video, "Dhar Mann Only Gets Worse."

Trivia

His "When A Karen Gets Arrested" video has been viewed more than 800,000 times. Outside of long-form content, his YouTube shorts have also racked up millions of views. In March 2023, he posted a photo on Instagram to celebrate reaching over 1 million subscribers on YouTube.

Family Life

He was born in the United States. His birth name is Dylan Stratton.

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow content creator Ben Azelart.
