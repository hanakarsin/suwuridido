King Cid - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-08

YouTube star whose prank and challenge videos have garnered him 2.1 million subscribers and 184 million overall views. His June 2021 video "SMASH OR PASS BUT FACE TO FACE YOUTUBERS EDITION!" featured Funny Mike and BentleyTv and has received over 7 million views. 

Before Fame

His first Instagram post was on January 26, 2018, and was a video of one of his friends jumping out of a moving school bus door to avoid going to school. He was cut from his high school basketball team three times. 

Trivia

He worked at a call center but quit his job when he reached 5,000 YouTube subscribers because he wanted to donate more time to YouTube. In November 2020, he released a challenge video called, "Last To Get Knocked Out In The Hood Wins A PS5!" which has received over 6.9 million views. 

Family Life

He has three siblings. His parents are from the Dominican Republic. His ex-girlfriend Breann Llewelyn appeared on his YouTube channel.

Associated With

Tanner Braungardt and Jake Mitchell are also YouTube stars born in 2000.
