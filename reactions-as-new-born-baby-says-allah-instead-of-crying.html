Reactions As New Born Baby Says Allah Instead Of Crying

Publish date: 2024-07-03

A video making the rounds on social media has shown a new born baby making sounds which sounds like “Allah”.

The video which has now gone viral has left some people rejoicing over the incident as they claim its a proof of the existence of God.

However, some social media users have said that the baby seems to be in distress, hence the reason for not crying.
