Baylen Levine - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Social media personality who found fame as the creator of his self-titled YouTube channel. His prank videos such as Call of Duty in the Library and Car Wash Hostage have helped him earn more than 3.4 million subscribers. His YouTube channel was previously called Benitez. 

Before Fame

He uploaded his first YouTube clip titled Farting on People Prank in January 2018. 

Trivia

He often features a gas station employee named Mr. Khan in his videos. 

Family Life

He posted an Instagram picture with his parents on Father's Day 2017. His younger sister Lilah has had a popular Instagram account at lilahlevine. He regularly posts content with his cousin, Kyle Johnson. 

Associated With

In October 2020, he uploaded a YouTube video titled "Lil Yachty vs Baylen Levine!"
