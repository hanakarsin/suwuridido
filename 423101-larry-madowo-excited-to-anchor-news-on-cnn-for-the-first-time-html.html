Larry Madowo Excited to Anchor News On CNN For The First Time

Publish date: 2024-05-03
  • Larry Madowo's stint with America's CNN began in May 2021 when he joined the network as its Nairobi-based correspondent
  • Later in July, the journalist was promoted to be the network's international correspondent
  • A month later, he made his debut as a news anchor in the multinational outlet based in Atlanta, USA

Kenya's Larry Madowo continues to fly the country's flag high in the international media.

The accomplished journalist has always been open to sharing every move he makes in his career.

The latest development is his first appearance on CNN as a senior anchor, this coming a few weeks after his elevation as the outlet's international correspondent.

"New thing alert! I’m anchoring on CNN International from Atlanta for a little bit. Join me LIVE today and every weekday. New York 12pm | London 5pm | Nairobi 7pm | Delhi 9.30pm," said Larry.

CNN international correspondent

Last month, Larry was promoted from CNN's Nairobi-based correspondent to be the network's international correspondent.

The elevation came three months into him joining CNN from BBC in May 2021.

While joining the multinational network headquartered in Atlanta, USA, Larry restated his zeal and passion to tell African stories.

At BBC, he served as the outlet's North America Correspondent in Washington, where he covered some of the biggest world stories on the big screen, among them, the demonstrations that followed the killing of George Floyd and the USA general vote held in November 2020.

As CNN's international correspondent, Larry was charged with covering stories beyond Nairobi and Africa.

He fully set his foot in the international space when he joined the BBC in early 2018.

In 2012, he had worked with CNBC Africa in South Africa before returning to Kenya months later to work with the Nationa Media Group-owned NTV.
