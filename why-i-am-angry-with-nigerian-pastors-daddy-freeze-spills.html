Why I Am Angry With Nigerian Pastors

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Controversial Nigerian OAP, Ifedayo Olarinde, popularly known as Daddy Freeze has revealed the reason he is angry with Christians.

Naija News had reported about a pastor berating members who always feel that pastors and their families are using their congregations money to live a luxurious lifestyle.

She revealed that if a pastor’s wife should make a beautiful hairstyle some might feel the hair is a product of the finances they are pumping into the church.

The cleric asked church members with such thoughts to desist from them, adding that pastors also sew seeds from their pockets for things to get better for them.

Reacting to the development, Daddy Freeze, noted that it defies logic and reason that only 2 per cent of Nigeria’s population has 500k and above in their account despite being the home of some of the worlds largest auditoriums and wealthiest pastors.

He added that he’s not angry that pastors are wealthy, but that the members are poor.

“I’m not angry that pastors are wealthy; I’m just angry that you are poor. 

“It defies logic and reason that only 2percent of Nigeria’s population has =N= 500k and above, despite 50 per cent of Nigerian’s population being Christian.

“This same Nigeria has the worlds largest church auditoriums and some of the worlds wealthiest pastors!”
