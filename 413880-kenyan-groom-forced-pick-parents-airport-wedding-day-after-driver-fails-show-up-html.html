It is always a dream of every couple to have a great and smooth wedding. Some weddings are always full of drama, from suppliers giving substandard services to makeup artists leaving brides looking like clowns on their big day.

Joel Junior Papa, a Kenyan and Ronah Nakalanda, a Ugandan, walked down the aisle on May 15, 2021, but things didn't go well as expected on their wedding day.

Joel and Ronah started dating during last year's lockdown, and in April 2021, he proposed to her before her family and friends.

A few days later, the couple decided to walk down the aisle.

On their wedding day, Papa hadn't bought shoes for the occasion and luckily, a friend of his sorted him out.

Still, on his special day, Joel was forced to pick his parents from the airport. They were travelling from Kenya and were stranded at the airport after the person who was to pick them failed to show up.

The cars the couple had hired didn't show up, and luckily they got some vehicles to use for their wedding.

"I was a bit anxious but my parents' presence really motivated me. I remember breaking down when I was told an hour to the wedding, that the cars were not coming. My dad encouraged me to cry. He said, cry, you’ll feel better after," the groom told My wedding.
