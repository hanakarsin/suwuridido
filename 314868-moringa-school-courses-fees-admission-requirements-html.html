Moringa school courses, fees, and admission requirements

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Moringa School is a Nairobi based tech-centered educational institution providing learners with a diversity of courses at an affordable price. Behind 5-week prep programs and 5-month courses is a world-class coding centric institution with a greater focus on current employment trends. Moringa School syllabus is friendly and highly dynamic to suit the needs of young technological enthusiasts.

A Taiwanese-American businesslady, Audrey Cheng, is the brains behind this immaculate instruction center. Since its inception in 2014, Moringa has steadily grown to become a bridge for potential and passionate tech-minded persons in our generation. The school runs in partnership with Hack Reactor, experienced instructors, and several employers to provide quality education and guarantee entry into the career market. The fact that Hack Reactor is a leading Silicon Valley Coding School further wraps the reputation of Moringa School not only in Kenya but also across the continent.

Moringa School courses

Which courses are offered at Moringa School? The institution accepts its learners under a couple of tracks among them the following;

1. Moringa Prep

The package equips learners with data science and software development skills within five weeks for full-time learning and ten weeks for part-time learning. The program targets beginners with fundamental concepts in the field of computer science. Listed below are key learning in this level of study at Moringa.

  • HTML/CSS
  • GIT
  • JavaScript

There are several other fundamental learnings under this category that actively prepare learners for the respective core program. Application deadline for the next prep class is 20th September 2019, and the classes begin on 14th October 2019. Graduation for this intake will be on 15th November 2019.

2. Moringa Core

This is a five-month immersive program comprising 15 weeks of intensive studies. The program includes both mobile and web developments. Also included are more advanced computing studies among them cyber security. The course comes with a 4-weeks project period as well as professional development. Students selectively stand a chance to receive early enrollment scholarships at Moringa.

Data Science

The course creatively blends the application of software and algorithms to unearth details useful in making critical decisions. It takes 18-months to complete.

Software development

The course equips learners with research, design, and essential coding skills for home and business app development. It takes 15 months to complete.

Moringa fee structure

Moringa School has been in operation in the country for the past five years and has successfully managed to attract several learners for their world-class educational approach and highly affordable fees.

  • Data Science - KShs. 40,500 as prep fees, and KShs. 160,000 as core fees
  • Software Development - KShs. 40,500 as prep fees, and KShs. 160,000 as core fees

Interestingly, students that pursue the Prep Program at Moringa stand a chance to receive a discount of KShs. 20,000 when enrolling for their coe. For this reason, such students will only pay KShs. 140,000 either in Data Science and Software Development core studies.

Moringa School admission requirements

Who qualifies for admission at Moringa School? The institution's admissions department accepts learners on a more relaxed selection criterion to attract passionate beginner programmers, junior software engineers, and even those at the managerial level seeking to further their knowledge in web design technology. Take note of the following basic Moringa School requirements;

  • Learners must have a laptop
  • Learners under the core program must be available on a fulltime basis
  • Learners under the prep program can flexibly choose between a five-week full time and a ten-week part-time option
