#2015InReview: Top Celebrities Who Had Babies In 2015

Publish date: 2024-02-22

In 2015, there was a baby boom in the entertainment industry. Many celebrities and their partners had wonderful babies. Though their parents had celebrated their arrivals, Legit.ng is celebrating all babies had by Nigerian celebrities in 2015. 

1. Olamide Adedeji and Aishat Suleiman

Baddo as Olamide is fondly called was the first celebrity to have a baby in 2015. His longtime girlfriend, Aishat Suleiman, gave birth to a baby boy in January.

2. Brymo and Esse

The Arambe singer had a baby with his girlfriend Esse on March 31.

3. D’ Prince

Don Jazzy's younger brother and Mavin Records artist D’Prince had a baby with his girlfriend in June.

4. Davido and Sophia Momodu

The HKN singer had a daughter with his older girlfriend, Sophia Momodu, in May. Sophia is Ovation publisher Dele Momodu's cousin.

5. Flavour and Anna Banner

Flavour had a daughter with former Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria (MBGN) Anna Banner in August.

6. Mary Remmy and Jason Njoku

The Nollywood actress, Mary Remmy, was delivered of a baby in August. She is married Iroko TV boss, Jason Njoku.

7. Jim Iyke and Dana

Nollywood bad boy actor Jim Iyke had a son from his Lithuanian lawyer girlfriend on September 6.

8. Tiwa Savage and Tunji Balogun

Tiwa and Tee Billz, as her husband is popularly known, welcomed their first child together on July 22 at St Mary’s Hospital, London.

9. Omawunmi and Tosin Yusuf

Omawunmi and her husband had a baby boy shortly after legalizing their union. The boy was born on July 18 in the USA.

10. Karen Igho and Yaroslav Rakos

The Big Brother Africa (BBA) winner had a baby boy on July 4, 2015.

11. Dr Sid and Simi Osomo

The dentist turned singer had a baby girl with his wife, Simi, in May 14 at Cedar-Sinai Medical centre, USA.

12. Uche Jombo and Kenney Rodriguez

Though she lost her first pregnancy, Uche had a bouncing baby boy on May 28 in USA.

13. Adaeze Yobo and Joseph Yobo

The former Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria (MBGN) had her 3 child with international footballer and former Nigeria football team captain Joseph Yobo. They had a boy on March 31.

14. IK Ogbonna and Sonia Moralez

Nollywood fine boy and model IK Ogbonna had a son with his Columbian girlfriend Sonia on June 20. The couple later legalized their wedding weeks after the delivery of their son.

15. Timaya and Barbara

Timaya and his longtime girlfriend had a daughter together on July 15. This is the second child for the Egberi Papa 1 and his girlfriend.

16. Nadia Buari

If there is a celebrity pregnancy that caught fans by surprise, it was Nadia's. She was so secretive about her pregnancy that many found it hard to believe she had a set of twins in March. The news only sank in after the actress' father confirmed it. Many assumed the twin girls were for her once celebrated boo, Nollywood actor Jim Iyke but later had a rethink when Jim went ahead to have a son with his now girlfriend. Nadia has since revealed photos of the father of her twins.

17. Venita Akpofure and Terna Tarka

The beautiful vixen and AY’s Crib actress had a baby girl on April 9.

18. Yomi Black and Liz

Celebrity photographer Yomi Black and wife Liz had a baby boy on July 1 in USA.

19. Stephanie Linus and Linus Idahosa

The ex-beauty queen, model and actress had her first child with her husband, Linus Idahosa. The baby boy was born on October 24.

20. 2 Shotz and Precious Jones

The Nigerian rapper and his wife had their first child together on March 13 in Ireland.

21. DJ Neptune and Ese Imohiosan

Foremost Nigerian celebrity DJ had a baby girl with his wife Ese Imohiosan in June.

22. Odunlade Adekola and Ruth

Odunlade Adekola's wife Ruth gave birth to a bouncing baby boy on Monday, November 23 in Abeokuta. This is their fourth son together.

23. Elohor Aisien and Owen Aisien

The former Miss Nigeria UK, celebrity event planner and Elite Model Look Nigeria franchise owner welcomed a baby girl on Thursday October 29 in a UK hospital.
