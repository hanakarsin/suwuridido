Paris Anderson - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Instagram star who became known as the girlfriend of famed YouTuber Tanner Braungardt. The couple would later break up in the summer of 2017 and she went on to gain her own social media following. She has collaborated with multiple clothing, food, jewelry and dental brands by promoting their products on her Instagram page. 

Before Fame

Tanner posted a YouTube video in 2017 titled "Meet My Girlfriend" to confirm that the two were dating. She shared her first picture with Tanner on her Instagram on January 10, 2017. 

Trivia

Her popular paris.anderson Instagram account accrued over 380,000 followers before it was disabled. She has since rebuilt her following of over 250,000 with her lifestyle and outfit photos. 

Family Life

She is originally from Augusta, Kansas and has a younger sister.

Associated With

She and Arzaylea both rose to fame on Instagram due to their romantic relationships.
