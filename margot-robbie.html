Margot Robbie Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Australian actress and producer Margot Robbie started her acting career in 2008 in the television series Neighbours. After relocating to America in 2011, she had her career breakthrough in the film Wolf of Wallstreet, which was nominated for five Oscars in 2014. Following this success, she gained even more fame for her roles as Jane Porter in The Legend of Tarzan and Harley Quinn in the DC Extended Universe films, beginning with Suicide Squad in 2016.

Margot has since co-founded the production company LuckyChap Entertainment alongside her husband, Tom Ackerley.

Full Name: Margot Elise Robbie

Birthday: July 2, 1990

Relationship Status: Married to Thomas Ackerley

Instagram: @margotrobbieofficial

Net Worth: $40 million

Fun Fact: Margot has always been an ice hockey fan, and she joined an amateur team upon moving to the U.S.
