'Its Not Your Business' Angel Reacts To Pregnancy Speculations

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Big Brother Naija reality TV star, Angel Smith, has asked womb checkers speculating if she is pregnant to mind their business and leave her alone.

This is coming after a selfie showing the protruding tummy of the ex-Shine Ya Eye housemate sparked pregnancy rumours, which led some Nigerians to believe she was expecting a baby.

Others fans berated the housemate for exhibiting a big tummy despite representing a waist training brand.

Reacting to the criticism, Angel, in a post shared via her Instagram story cautioned fans, she asked them to stop commenting on people’s weight as it is not their business.

Her post reads:’ Stop commenting on people’s weight. It’s not your business.’

It would be recalled that BBNaija Angel Smith took to social media to reveal that she has been so uninterested in life lately.

She said she is no more interested in romance, writing, texting friends but all she does is be in bed.

Her post reads “Been so uninterested in life lately. Not interested in romance( even though men have always been boring to me), not writing anymore, not texting friends, just in bed tired and overwhelmed. No social/love life.

“Hopefully I get out of the loop. I’ll blame it on the illness.”
