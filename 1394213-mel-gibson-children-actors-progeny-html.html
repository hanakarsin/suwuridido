Mel Gibson is a legendary American actor, film producer, director, and screenwriter. He is popularly known for directing and appearing in action movies. Apart from being one of the most successful and richest Hollywood stars, he is also a family man with amazing parenting skills. Would you love to know who Mel Gibson children are?

Parenting is not an easy task, as many may perceive it to be. It even becomes more challenging when one has a highly demanding job like that of actor Mel Gibson. At the time of writing, his eldest child is 40 years old while the youngest is 3 years old.

Who are Mel Gibson children?
