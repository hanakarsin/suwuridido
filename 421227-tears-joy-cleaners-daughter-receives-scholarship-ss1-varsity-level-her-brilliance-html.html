Tears of Joy as Brilliant Student Receives Scholarship from High School to University

Publish date: 2024-03-19
  • A Nigerian pastor, Juliet Olele, has put a smile on a woman's face after paying her daughter's school fee
  • Pastor Olele gave the 13-year-old girl a scholarship from SS1 to university level for scoring 8 As and 2 Cs in her Junior School Certificate Examination
  • The Nigerian pastor assists the needy through the Comforter's Hand of Care Foundation which she founded

A Nigerian pastor, Juliet Olele, has fulfilled her promise after giving a cleaner's daughter scholarship from high school to the university level.

Pastor Olele was impressed that the young girl, Favour Patrick, got 8 As and 2 Cs in her Junior School Certificate Examination.

How it started

In a video that Pastor Olele shared on her Facebook page, Favour and her mother expressed gratitude to God and their benefactor after the cleric announced the scholarship in church.

How it's going

Pastor Olele visited the teenager's school to pay her first tuition fee of N38,000 (KSh 10,022) through the Comforter's Hand of Care Foundation.

Favour and her mother embraced Pastor Olele when she stepped into the building, saying they were happy to see her.

Speaking with a member of staff of the school, Pastor Olele said:

"My name is Pastor Juliet Olele, by the grace of God I'm also the founder of Comforter's Hand of Care Foundation."

Tears of joy

Favour's mother could not stop crying when narrating how she struggled to make sure that her daughter went to school.

She thanked God for sending her a helper and also expressed gratitude to the school for the assistance they rendered her in the past.

Favour also appreciated her benefactor and prayed for the foundation.
