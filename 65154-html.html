Mercy Johnson &amp; Chika Ike React To Reports Of Being Enemies

Publish date: 2024-03-10

There were rumours making rounds that top Nollywood actresses Chika Ike and Mercy Johnson Okojie were at war with each other over respect issues.

Mercy Johnson and Chika Ike

According to the report, Mercy doesn’t like Chika because she doesn’t respect her as a top actress while Chika was said to be sad because the ban on Mercy was lifted.

The movie stars took their twitter accounts to joke over the report, saying Mercy's daughter Purity would be the referee during their fight.

Recall that Chika Ike was one of Mercy Johnson's Bridesmaid during her wedding.
