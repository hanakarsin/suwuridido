Popular Nigerian actor, Yul Edochie, recently took to social media to speak about online trolls and why he was oblivious about them.

The movie star took to his verified Instagram page to share a photo of his beautiful family during the holidays and explained that they were the reason he did not have time for trolls.

According to him, people should not feel bad when he does not respond to their insults.

He said that he was too busy taking care of the beautiful family God blessed him with.
