Ma Dong-Seok - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-04

Korean film and television actor best known for the films Train to Busan, One on One, and Murderer as well as the television series Bad Guys. In 2019, he was cast in the superhero film The Eternals starring Angelina Jolie. He has amassed over 50 credits throughout his ongoing acting career. 

Before Fame

He attended Columbia State University. He was a trainer of MMA.

Trivia

His Twitter account became inactive after amassing nearly a quarter of a million followers. He played the character MA Seok-do in the 2022 film The Roundup.

Family Life

He was born in South Korea. He started dating Ye Jung-hwa in 2016.

Associated With

He made an appearance on the Netflix series Sense8, which stars Aml Ameen.
