Jadon Sancho To Undergo Man Utd Medicals, New Shirt Number Revealed

Publish date: 2024-06-29

Borussia Dortmund midfielder, Jadon Sancho, is set to join English Premier League side, Manchester United, and will wear the No. 7 shirt at Old Trafford.

The deal between the two clubs is believed to be worth the sum of £72.6million.

The England player is expected to sign a five-year deal with the Red Devils, according to The Athletic.

The transfer would see Sancho emerge as the second most expensive English player in history, only behind his future club team-mate Harry Maguire, who cost £80m.

The 21-year-old will wear the No. jersey at Old Trafford, which was made famous by George Best and has also been worn by Bryan Robson, Eric Cantona, David Beckham and Cristiano Ronaldo.

The midfielder who is currently part of England’s squad at Euro 2020, will now undergo a medical before his official unveiling as a Manchester United player.
