Jane Mugo Holds Colourful, African-Themed Birthday Party for Son: &quot;I Feel so Lucky&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-30
  • Private detective Jane Mugo held a colourful party for her son as he turned a year older
  • The young boy looked dapper in an all-white outfit with African print
  • Mugo hired DJ Shiti as the MC for the party, which was held at a swanky resort in Nyeri

Famous Kenyan private detective Jane Mugo hosted a colourful birthday for her lastborn son.

On Tuesday, August 24, Mugo shared images of the celebrations held over the weekend on social media.

The young lad was dressed in an all-white outfit accentuated with African print on the wrists and neck in the images. Sunglasses complemented the young boy's superb look.

Mugo's son arrived at the party in style, as a red carpet was laid down for him.

Mugo also looked dapper as she was dressed in an African attire matching with her son's.

MC DJ Shiti

Among the attendees at the colourful celebrations was comedian DJ Shiti, who served as the MC.

The young boy's friends were also spotted at the celebrations held at a luxurious resort in Nyeri.

In the caption, Mugo expressed joy saying the celebrations were a success.

“I can’t really type into words how much I loved this past weekend celebrating my son’s birthday to perfection.It was so amazing to get away with a group of few friends and relax, hang out and chill. It was such an amazing treat. I hope everyone was able to have a wonderful weekend. I feel so lucky and so grateful” the private eye wrote.
