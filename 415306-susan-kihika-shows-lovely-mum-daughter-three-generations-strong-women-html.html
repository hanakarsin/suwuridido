Susan Kihika Shows Off Her Lovely Mum, Daughter: &quot;Three Generations of Strong Women&quot;

Away from politics, Nakuru senator Susan Kihika is said to be a refined reincarnation of her father. Ambitious and a political firebrand, she has emerged as the iron lady of Nakuru politics.

Simply put, Susan is her father’s daughter. She is resilient, combative, charming, resolute, and elegant, but one thing that is different about her is, she is her mother's lookalike.

The senator resembles her and has a daughter who is her copycat, making them three women who look alike as per a photo shared on her Facebook page.

"Three generations of strong women, My mama, and my baby," she captioned a photo of three of them.

Kihika, who served as a speaker in the Nakuru county assembly from 2013 to 2017, described her mother and daughter as kind hearts, fierce minds, and brave spirits.

The three women were praised by Kihika's fans and supporters who flocked the comment section to leave kind words for them.

Susan Kihika's divorce

The Nakuru senator is a mother of two, Tiffany and Ashley.

Kihika, who is among Kenya’s most outspoken female politicians, is also the president of the IPU bureau of women parliamentarians.

She was married to Sam Kamau Mburu, a billionaire and businessman.

In 2018, she filed for divorce after a five-year marriage when Mburu was arrested in a KSh 64 million fraud case.
