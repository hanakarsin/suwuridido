Tyler Perry gives KSh 2.2 million tips to 42 Atlanta restaurant workers during lockdown

Publish date: 2024-04-25

- The Movie mogul tipped 42 local Atalanta, US restaurant employees, with over KSh 2.2 million during a visit

- Each restaurant worker was to go home with KSh 50,000 which will go a long way in these tough times

- The movie producer has been seen coming into the restaurant for several pickups ever since the coronavirus pandemic started

During this period of the coronavirus pandemic, every little deed of kindness and generosity is welcome.

Much is expected and much has been done from top personalities and people like Tyler Perry continue to do more.

The Movie mogul tipped 42 Atalanta, US local restaurant employees, with over KSh 2.2 million during a visit.

Tyler is said to be a regular at a certain Houston's restaurant in Atlanta and was visiting to pick up his order, according to TMZ.

He decided to leave a tip of Ksh 50,000 for every worker in the restaurant which will definitely go a long way in helping their families at these tough times.

It is said the movie star has been seen coming into the restaurant for several pickups ever since the coronavirus pandemic brought several restrictions.

In November 2019, during a trip to Mexico, the movie producer settled a man's Ksh 1.4 million hospital bill.

Stephen Johnson and his fiancée Tori Austin had been on a dream trip in Mexico when things went horribly wrong while on a cruise ship.

Johnson went into diabetic shock and was rushed to a hospital in Progresso where he fought for his life in ICU for three days.

Things were about to get worse, with the hospital reportedly refusing to allow the US citizens to leave its premises, until the hefty bill was cleared.
