Shusho has not only been moving the masses with her music but also her flawless beauty.

In fact, some if not many of her fans usually watch her music video just but to enjoy the beauty that she comes with.

Well, the gospel musician recently decided to put her fans to task by asking them if she looks like American star musician Janet Jackson.

The celebrated musician shared a photo of herself on Instagram and personally noted that she thinks she resembles the star.

''Eti ni kweli nafanana na @janetjackson. Sijui yeye anasemaje?'' Shusho captioned.

Her post got the attention of her followers who thronged the comments section to give their answers.

Many agreed with her that indeed she looks exactly like Janet Jackson.

Some, however, especially men swayed off the topic and tried to lure her into their arms with quite a number begging her to side chat them on DM.

Shusho's post comes just months after she shared about how petrified she was when her Instagram followers number hit 666k mark.

TUKO.co.ke earlier reported that Shusho celebrated clocking 666k followers but her concerns were around the number which has always been attributed to the devil.

The Bible calls the 666 number the 'Unholy Trinity' and anyone having it is associated with the devil and the coming of the false prophecy.

Shusho jokingly protested about the number on her Instagram, asking her followers to share her handle for more follows to have the '666' number ejected.

"Guys, I am requesting you to change this number for me dear ones. 666 is not a good number. Please help me tag people," she said.

She went ahead to appeal to her followers' initiative to help her cross the 'difficult period'.

"Let the intentional procedure be done to cross me during this difficult period of this number guys. I pray for you so much," she added.
