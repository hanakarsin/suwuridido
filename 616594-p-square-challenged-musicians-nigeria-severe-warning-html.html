P-Square Challenges Musicians In Nigeria, Issues Warning

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Following speculations of a rift P-Square has addressed this as well as sent a strong warning to other artistes.

In an exclusive with Vanguard the brothers talked about how God made their dreams become a reality through music. They also acknowledged that everything started out as fun but eventually became a career.

The twins also credited their fans for the support in getting them to super star status, adding that they are ‘super fans’ without whom the journey wouldn’t have been easy.

Addressing reports of competition from other artistes the brothers said they are the only competition they see.

“We don’t have any competition; we only compete with ourselves. If you’re competing with P-Square, you’re wasting your time,” they said.

Recall that word on the street was that the brothers were splitting following series of speculations especially after Peter launched his reality show, Glo Dance With Peter.

With Paul joining the show recently as a guest judge they have further doused the many suspicions and speculations of a crack in their relationship.
