The reaction left many in stitches, especially men who appear to be the victims of blue ticks and being left on seen.

However, there are obviously other men who are not even thinking of Valentine's Day as they prepare to attend the highly anticipated Men's Conference which is hosted on the same day annually.

Hopefully this year it does not get postponed like the previous one which was brought forward after much deliberation.

The agenda of the much-hyped convention was arrived at following intense debates between organisers and the Kenya Film Classification Board (KFCB) CEO Ezekiel Mutua.

In what was a heavy blow for the men-folk, Mutua disclosed a steering committee settled on a date before Valentine's Day.

A statement by the KFCB boss, who was among the guest speakers, noted the decision was necessitated by the need to put in practice the resolutions by taking lovers out for a treat on lover's day.
