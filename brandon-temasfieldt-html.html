MessYourself - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-15

YouTube sensation who has more than 6 million subscribers to his channel. His videos feature comedic moments told through the perspective of well known video games. He has also begun posting his reactions to viral videos and other internet content. 

Before Fame

He studied mathematics at Uxbridge College but later decided to pursue his career as an internet personality.

Trivia

Some of the video games he features in his videos include Grand Theft Auto 5, Far Cry 3, and Infamous Second Son. He could frequently be seen posing with a big smile on his messyourself Instagram account which has gained over 230,000 followers. 

Family Life

His real name is Brandon Temasfieldt. He was born in England and is of Indian descent. He has two sisters: Candice and Frankie. He began dating YouTuber CrayisTaken in 2018.

Associated With

He started to gain a bigger following when fellow YouTube star Felix Kjellberg awarded MessYourself in his shout out competition.
