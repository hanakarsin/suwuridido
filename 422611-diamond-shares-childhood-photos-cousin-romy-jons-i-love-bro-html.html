Diamond Shares Childhood Photos of Himself and Cousin Romy Jons: &quot;I Love You Bro&quot;

Publish date: 2024-04-17
  • Diamond shared throwback photos of himself and his cousin DJ Romy Jons on his Instagram account
  • Apparently, the singer and his cousin had an inseparable friendship in their childhood despite their humble background
  • Diamond expressed brotherly love to Jons who celebrated his birthday just two days ago, on Saturday, August 14

Diamond Platinumz and his cousin Romeo Abdul Jones alias Romy Jones were apparently inseparable childhood cousins.

The two have always been mistaken by many for being blood brothers.

The Tanzania Bongo star shared series of childhood photos of himself and his cousin who is almost his age, Romy Jons on his Instagram account.

Diamond expressed brotherly love to his cousin who recently turned a year older.

"You know how much I love you, brother," Diamond captioned the photos.

Replying to the post, Jons who is Diamond's official Deejay said he loves his cousin, Diamond, too.

On his birthday, they partied in the night as they celebrated their success together and the journey they have been on.

The throwback photos show the transformation in Diamond and Jons and they prove that they have come a long way.

Many fans were surprised by their transformation and their bond.

Diamond has always treat Jons as his blood brother and many people know they are blood brothers.

Diamond Shows Up at DJ Jons Birthday Party

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Diamond shared a video showing off his incredible car collection, including his Escalades and the new Rolls Royce on social media.

In the clip, Diamond and Jons could be seen donning ski masks, with the singer's having silver jewellery attached to it. Jons matched his marron one with the colour of his suit.

The Jeje hitmaker then went from the first to the last posh rides claiming he did not know which car to show up to the party with. He finally decided to pick a cool bicycle stationed at the end of the 'car display' and use it instead.
