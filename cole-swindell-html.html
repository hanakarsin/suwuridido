Cole Swindell - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-31

American country music songwriter and recording artist known for the hit song "Chillin' It," which peaked at the number 2 spot on the US charts. He was named New Artist of the Year at the 2015 ACM Awards.

Before Fame

He was a member of Sigma Chi while at Georgia Southern University.

Trivia

He released a digital EP entitled The Down Home Sessions in 2014.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Bronwood, Georgia. He grew up with two brothers. In May 2023, he got engaged to Courtney Little. He previously dated Kelly Kelly.

Associated With

He wrote Craig Campbell's "Outta My Head" and Luke Bryan's "Just a Sip".
