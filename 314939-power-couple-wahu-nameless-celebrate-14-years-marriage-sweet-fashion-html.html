Power couple Wahu and Nameless celebrate 14-year marriage in sweet fashion

Publish date: 2024-02-09

Music industry's royal couple Wahu and Nameless have marked a new milestone in their blissful marriage.

The pair celebrated their 14-year anniversary in style and with the sweetest messages ever.

In an adorable and heartwarming Instagram post, Wahu celebrated the fact that they have weathered the storm together.

The pair has laughed, cried and celebrated together for over a decade and made two beautiful girls in the process.

"It is our 14th wedding anniversary!!! Where did the time go! What can I say babe... We have laughed, cried, fought, made up, made money, lost money, agreed, disagreed and throughout this roller coaster called life. I have always known I can count on you," Wahu wrote.

Nameless branded his lovely lady as his ride or die and hoped for more years beside the lovely songstress.

"Weh!!!! 14 years si mcheso...Happy 14th anniversary teammate! Ride or die," Nameless penned.

He even shared a sweet video of their wedding day that brought back fond memories of the past.

Wahu and Nameless are arguably the most unproblematic couple in the music industry and not one negative word can be used to describe them.

They have been hailed for keeping their lives private and teaching youths how to love and maintain a healthy union.

Once during an interview, Nameless said there was a point he and his wife nearly parted ways but by God's grace they stayed put and made things work.

Congratulations to these two.
