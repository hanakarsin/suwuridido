Actors Frederick Leonard, Peggy Ovire Give Details Of Their Wedding Ceremony

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Nollywood actor, Frederick Leonard and his colleague, Peggy Ovire are set to hold their wedding ceremony.

Naija News reports that this is coming a few hours after Frederick went public with their romance as he celebrates Peggy’s birthday on his social media page.

In an interview with LIB, the couple revealed they will be holding their traditional wedding ceremony on November 19, 2022.

The wedding ceremony will also hold in Warri, Delta state.

My Love For You Has Remained Constant

Frederick Leonard who has been hiding his relationship sparked mixed reactions with his birthday message to Peggy.

The actor expressed love for Peggy stating that his love for her has remained constant over the years and he would celebrate her always.

He wrote: “To Have n To Hold..

“To Love n To Cherish..

“In Good Times and in All Times..

“Through The Years, My Love For You Has Remained Constant..

“Look How Far We’ve Come Baby.

“It’s Your Birthday Today and So the World Celebrates You..

“But Our Love.. I Will Celebrate Always.

“Happy Birthday @peggyovire

“My Baby My Valentine..
