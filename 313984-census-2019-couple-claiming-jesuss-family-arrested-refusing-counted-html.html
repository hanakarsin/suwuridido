Couple claiming to be Jesuss family turn census officials away, says they were counted in Bible

Publish date: 2024-05-20

Police in Kitui county have arrested two people for allegedly refusing to cooperate in the on going National Population and Housing Census.

The man and his wife claimed they belong to the genealogy of Jesus Christ and should not be enumerated and included in the exercise.

When asked to give details, the couple turned hostile claiming they were already counted in the Bible thus needed no further enumeration.

The two were taken to Ndolos Police Station where they were locked up awaiting arraignment

In a similar but separate incident, another man was arrested but later released for the same offense.

The third suspect pleaded with the police saying he had changed his mind and was ready to give his details to census officials.

The arrested individuals belong to a cult known as Kavonokya.

The cult prohibits its followers from getting medication claiming they can be healed of ailments through prayers.

Sometime back, the sect members attacked and chased away health officials administering polio vaccine to children.

Story by TUKO.co.ke correspondent in Kitui county.
