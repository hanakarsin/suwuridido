US election 2020: Joe Biden's 50-point plan for America

Publish date: 2024-04-23

- Former vice president Joe Biden is running for president to rebuild the backbone of America which is the middle class

- Biden, the Democratic Party flag bearer, is also working towards ensuring everyone joins him in rebuilding America

- He said he understood the middle class was not just a number but a set of values

When former vice president Joe Biden formally announced his entry into the 2020 presidential race, he declared that he stood for two things.

They include workers who built the United States of America (USA) and values that could bridge divisions in the country.

Biden's pitch has been to create new economic opportunities for workers, restore environmental protections and healthcare rights and international alliances.

Here is the 50-point plan the former vice president has for America as outlined on his website, joebiden.com

2. Racial equity across the American economy.

3. Enact more than one-dozen middle-class tax cuts that will finally give working families the financial support they deserve.

4. Providing 21st-century caregiving and education workforce

5. Sustainable infrastructure and a clean energy future

6. Ensure the future is made in all of America by all of America workers

7. Full participation and equality for people with disability

8. Pursuing an aggressive and comprehensive plan to further women’s economic and physical security and ensure they can fully exercise their civil rights

9. Five-step roadmap to support local decision-making on reopening schools safely and to help students whose learning was interrupted

10. Provide leadership, stability, and trusted experience in the midst of a health crisis

11. Set up support for deserving small businesses

12. Give health care workers the long-term support they deserve including raising wages, guaranteeing quality, and affordable health care
