Kenyan woman sets herself on fire in a nasty domestic quarrel

Publish date: 2024-02-26

- A 28-year-old woman is currently undergoing treatment after setting herself on fire in what was understood to be a nasty love triangle

- She had quarrelled with her husband who had not come home for a night and kept calling another woman when he returned home 

-  The  lady, identified as Cynthia Njoki doused herself with kerosene and set herself ablaze 

A 28-year-old Cynthia Njoki is nursing 45% burns on her body after setting herself on fire  in what has been termed, a love triangle gone horribly awry.

The aggrieved woman reportedly set herself ablaze in a reaction to her husband's never-ending extra-marital affairs.

She sustained serious burns on her hands, abdomen, thighs and face after dousing herself with Kerosene and setting herself ablaze after a nasty tiff with her man.

Njoki who lives in Lang'ata told  rescuers that her husband had failed to come home on Saturday night and when he returned on Sunday evening, he was constantly calling a woman who he is suspected to be having an affair with.

Pained by his actions and overcome with anger, Njoki went to the bedroom,  poured kerosene on herself and lit herself up.

Nurses at the Rift-Valley Provincial hospital say that she was brought to the hospital at around  8pm on Saturday night in critical condition. Her fingers were burnt badly as were her thighs, abdomen, and face.

Doctors are conducting tests on her to determine the condition of her pregnancy. They had noticed that when she was brought in, her foetus's heartbeat was irregular.
