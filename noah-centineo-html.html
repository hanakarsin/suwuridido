Noah Centineo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Actor best known for portraying Dallas in Disney's Austin and Ally and later Jesus Foster on ABC's The Fosters. He also played Jaden Stark in the 2014 Disney TV movie How to Build a Better Boy. In 2018, he starred in Netflix's To All the Boys I've Loved Before as well as landed roles in The Perfect Date and Sierra Burgess Is a Loser. 

Before Fame

He made his acting debut at age 13 in the 2009 film The Gold Retrievers. 

Trivia

He has also acted in the series Marvin Marvin and Shake It Up. 

Family Life

He was born to Kellee Janel and Gregory Vincent Centineo. He has a sister named Taylor. In late 2020, he began a relationship with Stassiebaby. He dated Alexis Ren for about a year before they broke up in early 2020. He also dated Angeline Appel and Lauren Kolodin. 

Associated With

He acted alongside Bella Thorne on the Disney series Shake It Up in 2013.
