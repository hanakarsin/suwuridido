Woman Claiming to Be Pregnant Busted for Hiding Gun Inside Stuffing Pouch

Publish date: 2024-02-26
  • A woman was arrested after faking being pregnant when police stopped her
  • The cops searched her and discovered she had hidden a gun in her bulging stomach
  • The police also found several rounds of ammunition

Police officers apprehended a woman with a gun which she had disguised as a pregnancy.

According to Fox 2, the woman was driving in Michigan, US, when police officers on patrol stopped her for a traffic violation.

She informed the police that she was pregnant, and they were shocked after conducting a search on her.

The woman was hiding a semi-automatic rifle in her stomach, concealed in a stuffing pouch.

A picture taken at the scene depicted the gun with several rounds of ammunition and a massive piece of cotton wool where the weapon was hidden.

The man in the passenger seat also admitted to possessing an illegal drug, and they were both apprehended.

Faking pregnancy

In other news, TUKO.co.ke reported that a woman who faked a pregnancy to obtain money collapsed and died in court.

Olivia Nassanga from Uganda was accused of obtaining money from Owen Kawuki by deception. She lied to him that she was pregnant with his child.

On the fateful day, Nassanga was in court for her bail hearing when she collapsed.

The deceased's mother blamed Kawuki for her death, saying:

"My daughter had reported to court for her take her bail where she was offered a drink by Kawuki, which we suspect contained some chemical substances that could have caused her death.”

She was rushed to the hospital, where she died. Kawuki was arrested over her death.
