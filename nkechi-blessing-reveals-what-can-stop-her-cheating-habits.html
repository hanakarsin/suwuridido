'I'm Born A Cheater And Play Girl'

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Controversial Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing has bragged about her inability to stay with one man in a romantic relationship.

This is coming barely a month after her messy breakup with Ekiti State politician and boyfriend Opeyemi Falegan, who complained about her infidelity.

In an Instagram live session, Nkechi Blessing said despite being in any relationship, she’s still single until a man puts a ring on her finger.

According to Nkechi Blessing, a marriage proposal is the only criterion that can make her stop cheating in a relationship because she is born a cheater and playgirl.

When questioned about what it will take a man to keep her, Nkechi Blessing said amidst her consistent rant about money, a man doesn’t need to have anything to keep her in a relationship.

She said: “I’m single until I’m married my darling until a man put a ring on my finger before I can stop cheating, yes I’m a born cheater and play girl, a man can actually control me without anything. People who know me know the truth about money, forget the fact I that I always talk about money”.
