Jackie Chandiru's manager denies rumours singer is dead

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Ugandan songstress Jackie Chandiru's one time manager Balaam Barugahare has come out to debunk claims musician is dead.

Balaam shared a video of himself seated next to Jackie and explained her situation while pleading with her fans and the public to come out and support her.
