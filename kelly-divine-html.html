Kelly Divine - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Adult film starlet who entered the industry in October of 2007 after previously working as a swimsuit and promo model.

Before Fame

She attended Catholic school growing up, but was kicked out of her all-girl Catholic high school before spending her final high school years in public school. She later attended beauty school, completed cosmetology school, and worked in a salon for six months.

Trivia

She appeared in Playboy Radio shows in 2010. She starred in the Showtime series Look: The Series.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Chester, Pennsylvania. She is of Italian and Irish descent.

Associated With

She appeared on a Playboy Radio show starring Jessica Drake.
