Irina Meier - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Model and cosplayer who is known for her middle earth themes as well as super hero designs. She both creates and constructs her unique outfits and shares the modeling shots with her over 1.2 million Instagram followers.  

Before Fame

She first began sharing her cosplay work to Instagram in October of 2014. 

Trivia

In addition to Instagram, she also runs a Patreon page where she offers prints, wallpapers, and additional material for the fans who continue to support her cosplay.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Odessa, Ukraine. 

Associated With

She has featured a cosplay she did of a character from the game Overwatch on her YouTube channel.
