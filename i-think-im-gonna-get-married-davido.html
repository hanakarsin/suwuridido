I Think Im Gonna Get Married&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-18

Nigerian superstar singer, David Adeleke popularly known as Davido has hinted on plans to get married soon, saying he’s tired of being a player.

The afrobeat star, Davido hinted on joining the club of married men as he shared some lines on his social media page.

Naija News reports the singer took to his insta story late on Thursday night and shared some Tik Tok videos with the caption “I think I’m gonna get married soon, cos…”

In what seems like the reason behind his decision to consider quitting bachelorhood, the DMW boss added that “I don’t wanna be a player no more.”

The last line he shared is a line from one of his hit songs, ‘Fall’.

Just about a week ago, the singer had declared that he wasn’t single like some of his friends and colleagues.

During an exchange with Peruzzi on Instagram, Davido had clearly stated that he is not single.

Before things appeared to have fallen out between them, everything looked set for a marriage between Davido and Chioma.

Though not officially married, the duo have been seen together again at some family and other public events, sparking suggestions that they may be back together.

They also have a son together, Ifeanyi, with Davido once declaring Chioma as his ‘assurance’ in a song that became a hit track.
