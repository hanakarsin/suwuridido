Brian Redmon - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-04

Internet star, actor and fashion model who has earned over 900,000 YouTube subscribers on his self-titled channel. Additionally, he also has more than 40,000 followers on Instagram. He has posted romantic advice videos, challenge videos, Q&A's and vlogs on his self-titled YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

Growing up, he was an athlete and played baseball, basketball, and football. He posted his first YouTube video, "10 Things Girls Don't Know About Guys," on August 6, 2014.

Trivia

He has always been involved in youth ministry as helping younger kids is a passion of his. He went as a creator to Playlist Live in 2020 in order to meet some of his fans. 

Family Life

He was born to Marge and Jim Redmon; his brother is Cameron. He grew up in Ohio and later relocated to California. 

Associated With

He has collaborated with fellow YouTuber Tom Phelan.
