SCOAN Leadership Tussle Escalates As Late TB Joshua's Wife 'Evicts Disciples' [Video]

Publish date: 2024-05-08

The wife of Prophet Temitope Balogun Joshua, aka T.B Joshua, Evelyn, have allegedly evicted some disciples of the late prophet from the Synagogue Church of All Nations, SCOAN.

According to sources, the late Prophet TV Joshua’s Wife carried out that eviction as part of moves to assume leadership of the church that has been closed since the burial of the cleric.

“These disciples asked to leave were those who disrespected Evelyn while her husband was alive.

“They sidelined her, It’s a powerful tussle,” the source told Daily Post.

Meanwhile, Evelyn Joshua has been appointed into the powerful board of trustees of SCOAN in line with the church’s constitution which prescribes a minimum of three trustees for the church.

Naija News reports that her appointment is on the strength of the judgment delivered on Thursday by Justice Tijjani Ringim of the Federal High Court, Lagos Judicial Division holding at Ikoyi.

The verdict was delivered in a Petition brought by Concerned and committed members of the church represented by Mr. Kola Kareem and Mrs. Moji Oguntoyinbo against the Incorporated Trustees of the Synagogue Church of All Nations (SCOAN); the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and Mrs. Evelyn Onyisi Joshua in a Suit No: FHC/L/CP/1109/2021.

The petitioners were represented by Emeka Ekweozor Esq; Chris Eme Esq with him E.G Onyejekwulum. Ossazu Owie represented 1st and 2nd Respondents; while O.N Chukwu appeared for the CAC

Recall that TB Joshua died on June 5, 2021, after a church service.

A few weeks after his death, the popular prophet was buried within the church premises in Lagos State.
