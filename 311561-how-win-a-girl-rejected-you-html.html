How to win over a girl who rejected you

Publish date: 2024-07-05

You are in love with this girl who seems to embody everything you wanted in a woman. There is one problem. She doesn't feel the same about you. How do you win over a girl who rejected you?

Many men perceive rejection as a downfall and downgrading experience. Thus, they try to prove their worth by winning the girl of their dreams back. Although not everything will be as you want in life, there are ways you can increase the chances of you winning her over again. Here are some of the best tips you can use to win over a girl.

1.Try to move on by getting your mind off the rejection

Moving on after being rejected by a girl might sometimes feel like climbing a vertical shaft with no stairs. However, it's the proper way to make your mind grow and live a happy life once more. Furthermore, you were someone before you guys met.

Am I the reason the girl rejected me? Why me? These questions might pop up daily. Engage yourself in different activities, which make you happy. It may be playing a soccer game, cooking with a friend or just hanging out so long as they take your mind off the depression. Once you can get over the entire feeling of rejection, you will be able to re-strategize and go back with a stronger game.

2. Don't show any motives of hunting her

She has already said 'no' or 'not now', and this answer might as well not have changed by now. Girls love attention when it matters. Give her space to do her things. She probably has her friends and things to do apart from acknowledging your presence.

3. Get a makeover

Get a female or male friend to be your fashion guru. He or she has to have a good sense of fashion. Get the grooming tips to spice up your appearance and get a new look. In case you have money, get a new attire with a hairstyle that takes the shape of your face. You won't be surprised to see the girl come back after rejecting you.

4. Turn your weaknesses into strength

Other than making yourself look elegant for no reason, you also need to work on your weaknesses. Stop acting like a coward and invest your time on how you behave in her presence. Most probably, you already know the reason why she said no. Act on that! It may be a difficult task, but since you agreed that you wouldn't back down, go ahead and pursue her again.

5. Create interests in what she adores and cares

A girl rejected me but wants to be friends? What can I do? Another sure way to win over a girl who rejected you is to try and engage in the activities she loves. At this point, you have no choice but to love what she loves. With this, you can end up clicking even better than before.

6. Turn her friends into your friends too

If you have decided not to take no for an answer, try and make her close friends your buddies. Ensure they enjoy your company and talks as you seal any loopholes that resulted in the rejection. Ask them about her preferences and plan on making the girl your priority.

It should be done in great discretion that she may not recognize or get suspicious. All your actions and words ought to be accompanied with some trust to change a girl's mind about rejecting you. After hearing a positive remark about you from people she trusts, a girl will probably change her perspective of you.

7. Agree with what she decides upon

There have been cases when a girl rejects you but wants to be your friend. Practically, this means no to any form of a romantic relationship. When situations like this arise, it is best to act normal to avoid ruining the little connection you have. Use this opportunity to make her want you more. Show her that you can as well be the best friend she ever met. Also, work on getting tips from friends on what to do if a girl rejects you but still likes you, as probably you are not the first guy to encounter this in your circle.

At some point, the girl you have fallen for will realize how fun you are to hang around. At this particular moment, she might opt to see you as more than a friend. When it happens, you can rest assured that she will give you some signs though not necessarily in spoken words. Therefore, be keen to understand what she speaks in silence.
