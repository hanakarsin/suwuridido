'Jersey Shore' Star Ron Ortiz-Magro Only Wants One Baby Mama

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Is Jen Harley pregnant again? Ron Ortiz-Magro revealed his girlfriend might be expecting another baby on 'Jersey Shore: Family Vacation' — but is she?

The gang is back together for Jersey Shore: Family Vacation — and Ronnie Ortiz-Magro drops a major bombshell on his unsuspecting roommates. 

"She's late," Ron says, sharing that his controversial girlfriend Jen Harley might be pregnant again. As expected, the cast is not too excited about the couple welcoming a second child together amid their relationship troubles.

We mean, Ron does have a black eye (allegedly courtesy of his GF) when he shares the news. 

"It's definitely crazy to think that Jen is pregnant again because we just never know where they are in their relationship," Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi tells cameras. "If they're happy, or if they are fighting in the public, or if she's dragging him in a car. I mean, I just want them to be good."

So, is Jen pregnant again?

This episode was filmed months again, and judging from Jen's most recent Instagram post, it's safe to say that she is not pregnant and it was just a false alarm. 

Rocking a sexy black one-piece, Jen shows off her fit physique (and baby-bump free body) on the social media platform. Along with being mom to her daughter, Ariana, with Ron, Jen also has a 13-year-old son, Mason, from a previous relationship. 

Are Ron and Jen still together? 

Despite their very public struggles, it seems the couple is still together and in a much better place in 2019. This past Father's Day, Jen gushed about Ron and his parenting skills. 

"Gym, tan, baby has been your ritual since Ariana has been born! You have been an amazing father since Day 1," she wrote. "You have always shared the load of getting up at night, changing diapers, bath time together, sick days, comforting her when she’s just not having a good day... We couldn’t ask for a better dad dad dad than you!" 

And Ron made it very clear on the MTV series that he does not see himself having more kids with anyone else. 

"I would rather have another child with Jen than have another child with another woman," he shared. "Why do I want to have three babys mom? It's like, let's have kids, get one babys mom, and then that's what it is [sic]."  

Jenni "Jwoww" Farley, however, has some very honest advice for the couple if they do decide to get pregnant again in the future.

"I feel like they need five years of therapy before another child," she said. "That's my opinion, I'm allowed to have it. This is America. But apparently, they are fine... as he is sitting there with a f--kin' black eye." 

Watch Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Thursdays at 8 p.m. EST on MTV.
