Erica Accuses Laycon Of Been Cold Towards Her

Publish date: 2024-08-22

An housemate in the Lockdown edition of the Big Brother Naija, Erica on Tuesday  condemned that attitude of her fellow housemate, Laycon.

Erica in her statement noted that Laycon has been cold and hostile towards her.

She disclosed this during a conversation with Laycon after their Airtel task.

She further stated that she does not mind Laycon keeping his distance from her but she’s not happy with his attitude towards her.

She said: “I feel like you are cold and hostile towards me.”

” I don’t mind the space from me but I’m not happy about your attitude.”

She gave instances where Laycon had acted in a mean way.

Laycon while reacting to her statement apologize while stressing that “it’s subconscious.”
