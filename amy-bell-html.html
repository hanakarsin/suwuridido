Amy Correa Bell - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-02

An actress since the age of 11, her film A Girl Like Grace was selected to be in the Los Angeles Film Festival. She is also a musician in the band SterlingandAmy and a foot model and style blogger at amylovesu.com. She performed at Coachella in 2015 with the band Hot Natured.

Before Fame

As a child, she wanted to be a comedian. She helped write her elementary school's theme song at 9 years old and was in her Jr high school's choir and theater group.

Trivia

She has written and performed Spanish songs on television with her brothers The Correa Bros. She joined the girl group Gyrlz Society and was signed to MCA Records at 16. The band broke up 4 years later due to the lead singer wanting to go solo.

Family Life

She was born in Mission Hills, California to a father of Puerto Rican descent and mother of Guatemalan and Japanese heritage. In 2004 she married actor Ricky Bell.

Associated With

She married singer Ricky Bell of New Edition and Bell Biv Devoe.
