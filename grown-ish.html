'Grown-ish' Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-03-03

Freeform's comedy Grown-ish is a spinoff of the series Black-ish. In this new chapter of life, Zoey Johnson must navigate college and traverse through all the expected experiences that come with it.

Zoey is excellently played by Yara Shahidi, and the show also stars actors Trevor Jackson and Francia Raisa.

Grown-ish was nominated for Outstanding Comedy Series in the 2021 Image Awards.
