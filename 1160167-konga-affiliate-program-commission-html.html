Konga affiliate program and commission

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The Konga Affiliate Program is very popular in Nigeria and rightfully is ranked in the top 10 most visited sites in Nigeria according to Alexa rating. Working with this company means receiving the commission for each group of sold goods directly to the bank account specified by the program participant upon registration.

What is an affiliate program?

Affiliate marketing is a modern tool used in online marketing for the mass promotion of products or services on the Internet. One of the essential points is that to participate in the program itself, there is no need to have your selling points on the Internet, such as a website or a well-known blog.

Almost every candidate who has applied for the program can become an active participant in it. In general, the process is as follows: you place particular links to the product on your page in the social network, and attract users, among which can be your friends, acquaintances, colleagues work to follow this link and to make a purchase.

Also, you have the opportunity to make bulk direct messaging with the help of e-mail. If a link or a posted banner that you placed was followed by anyone and a purchase was made, then Konga gives you a commission.

Konga Affiliate Program

The Konga Affiliate Program is a so-called affiliate/referral marketing program in which each of the registered participants can earn money on commissions from advertising specific products on the Internet. In general, this is a legitimate way of making money, in which you can stay at home or in any other place with access to the Internet.

Candidates who have been officially registered on the Konga Affiliate Program are offered to advertise products and attract buyers for the purchase using various tools such as a website, social networks and their different platforms to promote products. All that is necessary for this is that after your registration for participation in the program it should be officially confirmed.

Konga commission rates

The Konga Affiliate Program offers its members a different commission percentage for each of the product groups that they promote and sell. This Konga commission is paid if the goods were purchased through one of the links that the participant posted.

On average, the commission can go up to nine percent. After each purchase, this percentage in the form of cash commissions transfers at the bank account of Konga affiliates of the program and twice a month the money can be withdrawn from the account.
