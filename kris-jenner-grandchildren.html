Kris Jenner Has 13 Grandchildren — Let's Meet Them

"There's no better teacher on this planet for us," Kim Kardashian said about her mom.

By Melissa Willets

Jun. 6 2024, Published 12:52 p.m. ET

Kardashian-Jenner family matriarch Kris Jenner is herself a mom of six.

She had four children with her late ex-husband, Robert Kardashian, and two with her former spouse, Caitlyn Jenner.

Read on to find out the latest count of all of the Kardashian-Jenner grandkids, from the oldest, Mason Disick, to the youngest, Rocky Barker, as well as everyone in between!

Kourtney Kardashian gave Kris Jenner four grandchildren.

Kourtney Kardashian-Barker is of course Kris's oldest daughter, and she has four kids.

Her first three — Mason, 14, Penelope, 11, and Reign, 9 — are shared with the reality star's ex, Scott Disick.

Kourtney welcomed a son, Rocky, with her husband Travis Barker, in November 2023 after struggling to conceive.

Despite her joy over Rocky's birth, in an awkward moment on the family's Hulu show The Kardashians, Kris doesn't seem too jazzed by the name Kourtney chose for her youngest grandchild. Oops!

Kim Kardashian also gave Kris Jenner four grandchildren.

During her relationship with controversial rapper Kanye West, Kim Kardashian welcomed four kids: North, 10, Saint, 8, Chicago, 6, and Psalm, 4.

“I am a single mom of four. And it is wild,” Kim has said about her current status following her highly-publicized divorce.

But she also gives it up to her mom Kris for setting a good example for her and her siblings. "There's no better teacher on this planet for us," Kim said.

Khloé Kardashian is a mom of two.

Kris's third daughter, Khloé Kardashian, gave her mom two grandchildren.

The Good American founder's first child, True, was born in 2018. The reality star also shares a son, Tatum, who turns 2 in July 2024, with her ex, Tristan Thompson.

"My mom is the most amazing human being in the entire world!" Khloé has said about her kids' grandmother.

Robert Kardashian has one daughter.

Kris's only son, Rob Kardashian, shares a daughter named Dream with his ex, Blac Chyna.

Chyna spoke to People about how the former couple handles raising Dream, who was born in 2016, saying, "Everything is good on both ends, we are all family at the end of the day. Robert and myself are co-parenting the best we can and it's all love and positivity.”

The model added that it takes a village to raise a child, presumably including Kris in that group.

Kylie Jenner is a mom of two.

Kylie Jenner, the youngest of the Kardashian-Jenner brood, is a mom of two.

She shares children Stormi and Aire with Travis Scott.

Of course, fans will recall the hubbub over Kylie's son's name. They originally named him Wolf, but later changed their minds.

Kendall Jenner isn't yet a mom.

We don't know what Kendall Jenner's plans are for a future family, but as of now, she is the only one of Kris's kids who doesn't have children.

"When I was younger I used to say that by 27 I'd love to have kids. Now I'm past that and I feel like I'm still so young. I'm enjoying my kidless freedom," the 28-year-old told Vogue in May 2024.
