Ronnie Dunn - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-16

Performer who was the other half of country duo Brooks & Dunn with Kix Brooks and who went on to begin a solo career in 2011. He has since released 5 albums as a solo artist. His fourth solo project, Re-Dunn, contained over 20 tracks. 

Before Fame

He attended thirteen schools in his first twelve years of elementary and high school education; he studied psychology at Abilene Christian University.

Trivia

His first solo attempt, the 2011 album Ronnie Dunn, had a Billboard Hot Country Songs hit single, "Bleed Red." His 2013 song "Cost of Livin" was nominated for two Grammy Awards. 

Family Life

He fathered three children with his wife Janine.

Associated With

He and Brooks won numerous awards, including 2005's vocal event of the year for "Building Bridges," featuring Vince Gill and Sheryl Crow on background vocals.
