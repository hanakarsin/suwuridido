They are in every school, like the air we breathe. Teachers who touched our lives in ways that no other educator did.

Some become favourites for their exemplary skills in imparting knowledge. Others touched a soft spot in our hearts for offering a listening ear when no other would. While some remain etched in our minds for their humorous actions, sentiments and quips.

TUKO.co.ke fans relived some of the moments that made their primary and high school worthy attending courtesy of above-par educators.

The first category was of those gems who made learning easy by crafting an approach that even the slowest of student would follow without feeling lost.

Without such teachers, some students would have performed poorly in their subjects and, most probably, had an altered future.

They epitomize servant leadership, stepping into class fully aware that they are moulding people's destinies.

That is as far as the good news goes, as there exists a flipside to the accolades.

Every market has a mad man, so the old adage goes which means that even learning environments harbour educators whose minds oscillate between teaching and other unnecessary distractions.

At the end of it all, it is important to note that in one way or the other, all these teachers played a role in who we are in society today.
